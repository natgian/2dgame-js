STORY

Deep within the ancient Greenwood, where the trees whisper secrets and spirits still roam, a red-haired guardian watches over the land.

Her name is Nara. Armed with courage, her bow, and unerring aim, she protects the forest from a creeping darkness rising at its edges.

The balance is shifting. Shadows awaken. But Nara does not falter. She draws her bow.

The Greenwood will stand.
